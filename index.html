Mini Smart Fridge

For our school's yearly student project showcase, my friend and I developed a mini smart fridge. My friend was responsible for installing the electronics, while I wrote the code to control the fridge's various functions. The fridge's screen displays its current temperature, and there is a lock feature to prevent unauthorized access. Additionally, the fridge has two lighting modes: one that uses RGB lighting, and another that displays colors corresponding to the current temperature inside the fridge. Overall, our project turned out perfectly and we are proud of the results.

Other Noteworthy Projects
Pixel Aimbot

This app is designed to lock onto enemies on the screen without reading the game's memory. It works by analyzing the screen in real-time, identifying the brightest pixels, and calculating the middle of those pixels to determine the location of enemies. This process is repeated more than 240 times per second, allowing the app to provide fast and accurate tracking of enemies.
